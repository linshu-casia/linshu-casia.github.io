Shu LIN is an Associate Professor in the National Key Laboratory of Cognition and Decision Intelligence for Complex Systems (C<sup>2</sup>DL) at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) since 2025. His major research interests include <b>Combinatorial Optimization</b>, <b>Game Decision-Making</b> and <b>Large Language Models</b>. Before joining in CASIA in 2021, Dr. Lin received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Software and Theory and his B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Peking University.
